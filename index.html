About Solstice MUN

Solstice MUN is a premier student-led conference blending diplomacy and mythology. Delegates represent nations under the guidance of Olympian deities, addressing global issues through the lens of divine wisdom and human collaboration. Our mission is to empower youth to become visionary leaders through structured debate and ethical diplomacy.

Our Committees
