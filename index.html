About Solstice MUN

In the spirit of the ancient Greek councils where gods deliberated the fate of mortals and immortals alike, Solstice MUN brings together delegates to embody the wisdom, power, and diplomacy of the Olympian pantheon.

Our Divine Mission

To channel the wisdom of the gods in addressing contemporary global challenges, fostering diplomatic excellence and strategic thinking worthy of Mount Olympus itself.

Olympian Values

Justice like Athena, eloquence like Hermes, strength like Ares, and wisdom like Zeus guide our delegates as they navigate complex international affairs.

The Hero's Journey

Embark on an epic quest of personal growth, master the art of rhetoric, forge alliances, and emerge as a champion of global discourse.

The Olympian Councils

Each committee is presided over by the spirit of a Greek deity, guiding deliberations with their divine wisdom and domain of influence.

Council of Zeus

King of Gods - UNSC

The supreme council addressing matters of international peace, security, and the balance of power among nations.

Council of Athena

Goddess of Wisdom - UNHRC

Champions of justice and human rights, promoting dignity and equality for all mortals across the globe.

Council of Ares

God of War - DISEC

Strategic deliberations on disarmament, military tensions, and the pursuit of peace through strength and diplomacy.

Council of Demeter

Goddess of Harvest - ECOSOC

Nurturing global prosperity, sustainable development, and the equitable distribution of the earth's bounty.

Council of Apollo

God of Healing - WHO

Guardians of global health, combating disease and promoting wellness with the precision of the divine physician.

Council of Nemesis

Goddess of Justice - UNODC

Enforcing divine retribution against crime, corruption, and the underworld of illicit human activity.
